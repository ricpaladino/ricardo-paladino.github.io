Dr. Ricardo Paladino Marques de Paula

Orthopedic and Traumatology Specialist in Training

I am a physician with dual medical degrees obtained in Brazil (UFSC) and Italy (UNIPV) with top honors. I am currently a resident in Orthopedics at the University of Milan and regularly work in Italian medical facilities.

I have enriched my experience with national and international fellowships, working at leading hospitals in Italy, including Policlinico Gemelli in Rome, Policlinico San Matteo in Pavia, Ospedale Gaetano Pini and Ospedale Galeazzi Sant’Ambrogio in Milan.

I actively participate in conferences and professional courses, both in Italy and abroad, to stay up-to-date with scientific developments and innovative orthopedic techniques.

I am also dedicated to integrating Artificial Intelligence in Orthopedics, exploring advanced imaging techniques to personalize therapy, and advancing regenerative orthopedic medicine.

Currently, I focus primarily on the pathology and surgery of the foot and ankle, the knee, and in the traumatology field, aiming to offer patients a modern, safe, and personalized approach.
